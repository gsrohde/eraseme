Detailed Description

Calculates the amount of sunlight scattered out of the direct beam by the atmosphere. The result is expressed in two ways: as atmospheric transmittances (which would be appropriate for calculating the intensity of direct and diffuse light at the surface given a value for the solar constant) or as a fraction (which would be appropriate for calculating the intensity of direct and diffuse light given an experimental measurement of light intensity at the surface).
