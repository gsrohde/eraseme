Detailed Description

Calculates the change in plant organ biomasses due to senescence.

Intended to be used with the senescence_coefficient_logistic module.

The amount that each plant component is senesced is determined as a percentage (kSeneLeaf, kSeneStem, kSeneRoot, kSeneRhizome) of its current biomass. Remobilization of senesced leaf tissue is also included based on the partitioning growth parameters.
