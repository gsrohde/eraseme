Detailed Description

Calculates hourly plant development rate based on thermal time ranges of emergence, vegetative, and reproductive growth stages.

This module is designed to be used with the development_index module.

Based on growth rate functions from Osborne et al. and uses the following DVI ranges:

Development index (DVI) Growth stages
\( -1 \le \text{DVI} < 0 \) sowing to emergence
\( 0 \le \text{DVI} < 1 \) vegetative growth stages
\( 1 \le \text{DVI} < 2 \) reproductive growth stages

The development_rate, \( r \) is defined as:

\[ r = \frac{t-t_\text{base}}{TT_\text{stage}}, \]

where \( t \) is the temperature (temp), \( t_\text{base} \) is the base temperature (tbase), and \( TT_\text{stage} \) is the accumulated thermal time for each of the above three growth stages (TTemr, TTveg, and TTrep).

References:

Osborne, T. et al. 2015. “JULES-Crop: A Parametrisation of Crops in the Joint UK Land Environment Simulator.” Geoscientific Model Development 8(4): 1139–55.
